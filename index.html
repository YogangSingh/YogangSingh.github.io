Research Interests

For the last ten years, my inter-disciplinary research interest lies in Autonomous Shipping, Maritime Path Planning, & Multi-Robot Systems in Maritime Environment with a strong focus on sustainability, asset efficiency, operational costs and human-in-loop approaches. It is my firm belief that such research areas provide opportunities for finding a real-time solution to pertinent maritime issues How to reduce the operational cost of sustainable autonomous shipping approaches through existing research methodologies? How to effectively monitor pollution in the ocean using marine robots? How to enable autonomy in various forms of the dynamic maritime environment through shared situational awareness?
